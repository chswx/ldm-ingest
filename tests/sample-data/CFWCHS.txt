THE NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE IN CHARLESTON HAS ISSUED A COASTAL
FLOOD ADVISORY...WHICH IS IN EFFECT UNTIL 10 AM EST THIS MORNING.

* LOCATION...THE COASTAL SOUTH CAROLINA COUNTIES OF
  CHARLESTON...COLLETON AND BEAUFORT. THIS INCLUDES DOWNTOWN
  CHARLESTON. 

* TIDE LEVELS...WILL PEAK IN THE CHARLESTON HARBOR BETWEEN 7.0 AND 7.2
  FEET MEAN LOWER LOW WATER.

* TIMING...HIGH TIDE OCCURS AROUND 712 AM IN THE CHARLESTON HARBOR
  AND AROUND 814 AM IN BEAUFORT. 

* IMPACTS...INUNDATION OF SALTWATER IN AND NEAR LOW-LYING 
  COASTAL AREAS COULD LEAD TO SOME ROAD CLOSURES AND FLOODING OF 
  PROPERTIES.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

BE PREPARED FOR THE POSSIBILITY OF ROAD CLOSURES AND MINOR
FLOODING OF PROPERTIES. IF TRAVEL IS NECESSARY...DO NOT ATTEMPT
TO DRIVE THROUGH WATER OF UNKNOWN DEPTH.
