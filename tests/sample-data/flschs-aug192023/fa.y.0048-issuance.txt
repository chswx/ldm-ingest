...FLOOD ADVISORY IN EFFECT UNTIL 530 PM EDT THIS AFTERNOON...

* WHAT...Flooding caused by excessive rainfall is expected.

* WHERE...A portion of southeast South Carolina, including the 
  following counties, Berkeley and Charleston.

* WHEN...Until 530 PM EDT.

* IMPACTS...Minor flooding in low-lying and poor drainage areas. 

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...
  - At 406 PM EDT, Doppler radar indicated showers and

    flooding.

    Awendaw, Huger, Smith, Limerick, Charity, Richmond, Buck
    Hall, Woodland, Hagan, Woodland Landing and Comingtee.
