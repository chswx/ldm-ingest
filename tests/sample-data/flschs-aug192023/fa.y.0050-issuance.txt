...FLOOD ADVISORY IN EFFECT UNTIL 8 PM EDT THIS EVENING...

* WHAT...Flooding caused by excessive rainfall is expected.

* WHERE...A portion of southeast South Carolina, including the 
  following counties, Berkeley and Charleston.

* WHEN...Until 800 PM EDT.

* IMPACTS...Minor flooding in low-lying and poor drainage areas. 

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...
  - At 634 PM EDT, Doppler radar indicated showers and
    thunderstorms with torrential rainfall extending from Daniel
    Island to Whitehall Terrance, Awendaw and McClellanville. As
    much as 1 to 4 inches of rain has fallen over parts of the
    area this afternoon.
  - Additional rainfall amounts of 1 to 2 inches are expected
    over the area. This additional rain will result in minor
    flooding. Motorists traveling US-17 near Awendaw should
    remain alert for significant ponding of water on the road.
  - Some locations that will experience flooding include...
    North Charleston, Mount Pleasant, Goose Creek, Hanahan,
    McClellanville, Daniel Island, Downtown Charleston, Isle Of
    Palms, Awendaw, Garris Landing, Bull Island, Price Inlet,
    Capers Inlet, Mount Pleasant Regional Airport, Lighthouse
    Island, Wando, Cainhoy, Cape Romain, Boone Hall Plantation
    and Cape Island.


PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Turn around, don't drown when encountering flooded roads. Most flood
