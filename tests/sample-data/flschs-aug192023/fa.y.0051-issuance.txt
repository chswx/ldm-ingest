...FLOOD ADVISORY IN EFFECT UNTIL 8 PM EDT THIS EVENING...

* WHAT...Flooding caused by excessive rainfall is expected.

* WHERE...A portion of southeast South Carolina, including the 
  following county, Charleston.

* WHEN...Until 800 PM EDT.

* IMPACTS...Minor flooding in low-lying and poor drainage areas. 

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...
  - At 715 PM EDT, Doppler radar indicated showers and
    thunderstorms with torrential rainfall over Mount Pleasant,
    Isle of Palms and Sullivans Island. This line will build
    slowly south and southwest through 8 PM.
  - Additional rainfall of 1 to 1.5 inches are expected over the
    area. This additional rain will result in minor flooding.
  - Some locations that will experience flooding include...
    Mount Pleasant, Isle Of Palms, Sullivan's Island, Sullivans
    Island, Fort Moultrie, The Old Village, Remleys Point, Wild
    Dunes, Mount Pleasant Waterfront Park, Patriots Point and
    Ravenel Bridge.


PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Be aware of your surroundings and do not drive on flooded roads.
