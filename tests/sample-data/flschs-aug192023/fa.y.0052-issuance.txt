...FLOOD ADVISORY IN EFFECT UNTIL 915 PM EDT THIS EVENING...

* WHAT...Flooding caused by excessive rainfall is expected.

* WHERE...A portion of southeast South Carolina, including the 
  following county, Charleston.

* WHEN...Until 915 PM EDT.

* IMPACTS...Minor flooding in low-lying and poor drainage areas. 
  Overflowing poor drainage areas.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...
  - At 747 PM EDT, Doppler radar indicated a nearly stationary
    cluster of showers and thunderstorms with torrential rains
    over Mount Pleasant, Sullivans Island, Isle of Palms and
    Dewees Island. As much as 1 to 3 inches of rain has fallen in
    this area this evening.
  - Additional rainfall amounts of 0.5 to 1.5 inches are expected
    over the area. This additional rain will result in minor
    flooding. Motorists driving US-17 between the Ravenel Bridge
    and Whitehall Terrace should be on alert for flooded areas.
  - Some locations that will experience flooding include...
    Mount Pleasant, Isle Of Palms, Sullivan's Island, Mount
    Pleasant Towne Centre, Boone Hall Plantation, Sullivans
    Island, Capers Inlet, Hamlin Plantation, Seaside Farms, Mount
    Pleasant Hospital, Wild Dunes, Whitehall Terrace, Snowden,
    East Cooper Regional Medical Center, Dewees Island, Ion, Fort
    Moultrie, The Old Village, Hobcaw Point and Remleys Point.


PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Be aware of your surroundings and do not drive on flooded roads.
