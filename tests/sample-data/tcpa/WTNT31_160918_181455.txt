DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK
------------------------------
At 1100 AM EDT (1500 UTC), the center of Tropical Depression Julia
was located near latitude 31.8 North, longitude 77.1 West.  The
depression is moving toward the west near 2 mph (4 km/h).  A turn

is expected to continue into Tuesday.
