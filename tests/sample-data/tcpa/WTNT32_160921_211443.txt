DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK
------------------------------
At 1100 AM AST (1500 UTC), the center of Tropical Depression Karl
was located near latitude 20.8 North, longitude 57.8 West.  Karl is
moving toward the west-northwest near 9 mph (15 km/h).  A turn
toward the northwest with an increase in forward speed is expected
by this evening, and that motion should continue through early
Friday.

Maximum sustained winds are near 35 mph (55 km/h) with higher gusts.
Little change in strength is forecast during the next day or so,
but some strengthening is expected on Thursday.

Dropsonde data from the NASA/NOAA Global Hawk indicate that the
minimum central pressure is 1007 mb (29.74 inches).
