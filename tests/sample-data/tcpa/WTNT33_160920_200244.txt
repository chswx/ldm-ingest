DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK
------------------------------
At 1100 PM AST (0300 UTC), the center of Tropical Depression
Thirteen was located near latitude 14.2 North, longitude 29.0 West.
The depression is moving toward the west-northwest near 9 mph, and
this general motion is expected during the next couple of days.

Maximum sustained winds remain near 35 mph (55 km/h) with higher
gusts. Some strengthening is forecast during the next 48 hours, and
the depression is expected to become a tropical storm on Tuesday.

The estimated minimum central pressure is 1007 mb (29.74 inches).
