DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK
------------------------------
At 500 AM AST (0900 UTC), the center of Tropical Depression Lisa was
located near latitude 23.8 North, longitude 37.9 West. The
depression is moving toward the northwest near 9 mph (15 km/h).  A
turn toward the west-northwest and an increase in forward speed is
expected later today, before a turn back toward the northwest and
then north on Sunday.

Maximum sustained winds are near 35 mph (55 km/h) with higher gusts.
Some weakening is forecast during the next 48 hours, and Lisa is
expected to become a post-tropical cyclone by this evening.

The estimated minimum central pressure is 1010 mb (29.83 inches).
