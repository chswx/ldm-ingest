DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK
------------------------------
At 500 PM AST (2100 UTC), the center of Tropical Storm Ian was
located near latitude 27.0 North, longitude 52.7 West.  Ian is
moving toward the north-northwest near 16 mph (26 km/h), and a turn

gusts.  Some slight strengthening is possible during the next couple
of days.

from the center.
